## 🎮 Features In Depth

### ⚡ Generator (upgraded)

Five distinct name generation styles:

- **Compound** — Classic `Color + Animal + Suffix` (OG style)
- **Verb-First** — `Disrupt + Quantum + Protocol` energy
- **Fake Latin** — `Celerixum`, `Novixas`, `Temporalis` (sounds like a pharmaceutical or a Roman emperor)
- **Number Soup** — `42NarwhalChain`, `v3QuantumVault`
- **Cosmic** — Completely unhinged. No rules. No gods.

### 🌡️ Chaos Levels

- **Mild** — VC-safe. Won't scare the partners.
- **Medium** — YC-ready. Weird enough to be "interesting."
- **Extreme** — Confetti from 5 directions. Names that shouldn't exist.
- **Cosmic** — Unicorn glitches. Names from another dimension. Unlock with the easter egg.

### ⚔️ Battle Mode

Two names enter. One name leaves. Click the winner. The loser is struck from the record. Battle history is preserved for posterity and regret.

### 🔥 Roast Mode

Enter any startup name and receive a devastating, personalized roast. 20 different roast templates. Works on your own generated names or any name you type manually. Great for processing rejection.

### 📊 Pitch Deck Generator

One click generates a complete Series A pitch deck including:
- The Problem (everything is broken)
- The Solution (we have slides)
- Market Size (TAM: everything, SOM: realistically $12)
- Traction (beta users: 3, two are founders' moms)
- Business Model (TBD post-Series A)
- Team (CEO: dropped out of 2 universities)
- Financials (revenue: $0, vibes: infinite)
- The Ask ($5M at $50M pre-money, "based on vibes")

Copy the full deck to clipboard and paste directly into a VC email.

### 📜 History

Saves up to 100 names in localStorage. Load any past name back into the generator, copy, delete, or export the full list as a `.txt` file.
